Some commonly asked questions
  • How do I get my interest rate estimate? keyboard_arrow_right
    Use our handy loan rate calculator to find out what low rate you qualify for. We lend with a rate that reflects your credit history - the better your credit score, the lower rate you will pay. Using the calculator does not affect your credit score.
  • What do I need to apply? keyboard_arrow_right
    You will be required to supply basic personal details, such as your driver's licence, employment details, assets, liabilities and income. We will be in contact to verify this information with you later.
  • How long does approval take? keyboard_arrow_right
    You will be notified of the outcome of your application no longer than 24 hours after your application is submitted. Often, we are able to approve a loan within 2 hours, if all required information is complete.
  • How do I make repayments? keyboard_arrow_right
    Loan payment can be made by direct bank transfer, a direct debit agreement or you can make a voluntary payment whenever via PayPal.
  • How long do I have to pay off my loan? keyboard_arrow_right
    Depending on your approved loan amount and credit history, the available loan terms will vary. Our minimum term is 3 months and the maximum term offered is 48 months. Special circumstances can be taken into account if you are experiencing financial difficulty.
